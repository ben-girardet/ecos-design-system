Card

The ecos-card component is a visual container and design system provider. By default fast-card applies neutralFillCard to its background that is calculated from its parent design system provider. If a custom background color is desired the attribute card-background-color is available, this will reset the cards design system to that value. Cards are snapshots of content that are typically used in a group to present collections of related information.

This component is stricktly identical to its FAST version.
